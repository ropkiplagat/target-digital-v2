The System

How The AI Funnel Machine Works
— 6 Steps, All Automated.

Every step in your funnel — from the first ad click to the booked appointment — is handled by Claude Code agents running on Railway. Here's exactly what happens:

01
Lead Capture

When a prospect clicks your Meta or Google ad, they land on a conversion-optimised page. The AI captures name, email, phone, and intent in under 60 seconds — no long forms, no friction.

02
Instant Response

Within 90 seconds of submitting, the lead receives a personalised SMS and email from your brand. Not a generic autoresponder — a contextual message referencing their specific intent and ad source.

03
ICP Qualification

The AI asks 2–3 qualifying questions via SMS conversation. It scores the lead against your ICP criteria and tags them: hot, warm, or not-a-fit. Only qualified leads go to your calendar.

04
7-Touch Nurture

Leads who don't book immediately enter a 7-message nurture sequence across email and SMS — spaced over 14 days. Each message is personalised, relevant, and moves them closer to a decision.

05
Appointment Booking

When a lead is ready, the AI sends a direct Calendly link and confirms the booking automatically. It handles reschedules, sends reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before — and reduces no-shows by up to 60%.

06
Pipeline Reporting

Every lead, every message, every conversion is tracked. You get a live dashboard showing cost-per-lead, response rates, show rates, and revenue attributed — updated in real time, every day.
