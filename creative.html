What Is Andromeda?

In 2024, Meta deployed Andromeda — a new AI-powered ad ranking system that evaluates every ad impression individually. It learns which users have already seen your creative, which have ignored it, and which have scrolled past it three times. The more times it shows a fatigued creative to a disengaged user, the lower it ranks your entire ad account — not just that one ad. Creative burnout is no longer just an engagement problem. It's an algorithmic penalty that compounds across your whole campaign.

  • Creative fatigue in 7 days — the average Facebook ad loses 50% of its effectiveness within a week. Most businesses replace creative every 2–4 weeks. The gap is where your budget burns.
  • Production is the bottleneck — shooting new creative requires a photographer, actors, a brief, an edit, and approval. By the time it's ready, the previous creative is already dead.
  • Andromeda penalises repeats — showing fatigued creative to users who've already scrolled past it signals low relevance, suppressing your reach account-wide.
  • One winning angle isn't enough — a single hook that works gets exhausted fast. You need 10 angles, 10 formats, 10 audiences — tested simultaneously. That's impossible manually.
