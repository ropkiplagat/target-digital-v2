The Process

How The Outbound Engine Runs
— Step by Step.

01
ICP Definition

We map your ideal customer: job title, seniority, company size, industry, tech stack, geography. The more specific the ICP, the higher the reply rate.

02
Scrape + Enrich

PhantomBuster scrapes LinkedIn and directories daily. Apollo enriches every contact with verified email and intent data. New contacts are added to your pipeline automatically.

03
Verify + Clean

Million Verifier cleans every address before it touches your sending domain. Invalid emails are removed. Your sender reputation stays pristine.

04
Sequence + Send

Instantly sends a 4-step personalised sequence — initial email, two follow-ups, and a break-up — each with the contact's name, company, and a relevant hook. Subject lines A/B tested automatically.

05
Reply + Book

Positive replies are flagged immediately. Interested leads are routed to your Calendly. The AI handles objections in the first reply, increasing meeting conversion.
