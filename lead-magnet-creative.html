What Is Facebook Andromeda?

Andromeda is Meta's AI-powered ad ranking system. It doesn't just look at your bid — it looks at predicted engagement, creative quality scores, and historical performance of your entire ad account.

An account with fatigued creative pulls down the performance of every ad in the account. Pausing a bad ad doesn't fully fix the problem. The algorithm remembers.
