## 3. GAPS WE CAN EXPLOIT

### What they are NOT saying:
- **None name their specific tech stack.** "We use AI" is the standard. Nobody says "PhantomBuster → Apollo → Million Verifier → Instantly → Railway." Naming the stack = instant credibility.
- **None mention Facebook Andromeda.** Zero competitors name the specific algorithm by name. Target Digital owns this angle entirely.
- **None guarantee a deployment timeline.** "48 hours to live" is unclaimed positioning in this market.
- **None say /month pricing openly.** Every competitor hides pricing. Transparent pricing signals confidence.

### Pain points they miss:
- **The after-hours lead gap** — Podium touches on this but positions it as a SaaS tool. No agency is making it a narrative pain point.
- **Creative account fatigue costing you across all ads** — Competitors talk about "fresh creative" generically. Nobody explains the account-wide penalty mechanism (Andromeda).
- **The cost of a 1-star rating** — Podium mentions reviews but doesn't quantify the revenue gap per star. The Harvard Business School stat (5–9% revenue per star) is unclaimed.
- **The 70% follow-up failure rate** — No competitor leads with this specific stat. It's Target Digital's to own.

### Proof they lack:
- **Named individuals with before/after numbers.** Competitors use logos. Target Digital uses: Wilson W (43s→4s), Joan R (4.1→4.9 in 90 days, CPL -47%), Brijesh Singh (11 winners month 1, 3–8 calls/week). This level of specificity is unmatched locally.
- **Industry-specific proof.** Competitors show generic client lists. Joan = mental health, Brijesh = B2B robotics, Wilson = automotive. Cross-industry proof at SMB level.
- **Speed proof.** No competitor claims "deployed in 48 hours." Target Digital can.

### Positioning unclaimed:
- **"Brisbane's AI marketing agency for SMBs"** — Evolut owns enterprise. Podium is SaaS. Nobody owns the local SMB done-for-you AI agency position in Brisbane/QLD.
- **Product-specific, named systems** — AI Funnel Machine, Automated Outbound Engine, Creative Goldmine, AI Reputation Management. Named products with named stacks. No competitor does this at SMB level.
- **Stack transparency** — Publishing the exact tools used (PhantomBuster, Apollo, Kling AI, HeyGen, Claude Code) is unclaimed. Competitors treat their stack as a trade secret. Target Digital uses it as a proof of competence signal.
- **The "feeds your funnel" bundle angle** — The cross-sell story (Creative feeds the Funnel which feeds Reputation) is a unique connected narrative. No competitor positions multiple systems as a growth stack.
