The Problem

Most businesses think they have a traffic problem.

They don't. They have a leak problem.

70% of leads that land in your funnel never get a real follow-up. Not because your team is lazy — because the gap between "interested" and "replied to" is too wide for humans to close fast enough.

This audit shows you exactly where your funnel is leaking. And what to plug first.

If you find 3 or more leaks — your funnel is costing you real revenue every week.

The 7 Checks

Run through each one. Be honest.

01

The 5-Minute Rule

If you're not following up within 5 minutes of a lead coming in, you're losing them to whoever replies first.

Response rate drops 80% after the first hour. Most businesses reply in 6–18 hours.

Check: What's your average response time right now?
02

The After-Hours Gap

43% of leads come in outside business hours.

If you're relying on a human to reply, those leads are cold by morning.

Check: What happens when a lead submits at 9pm on a Friday?
03

The Single-Touch Problem

Most sales teams give up after 1–2 follow-ups. Most deals close on follow-up 5–8.

If your sequence has 2 touchpoints, you're stopping before the sale starts.

Check: How many automated touchpoints does your sequence have?
04

The Wrong Channel

Email open rates: 20–30%. SMS open rates: 98%.

If you're only following up by email, you're reaching one in five leads.

Check: Does your funnel use SMS?
05

The No-Qualify Filter

Unqualified leads eat your team's time.

If your funnel isn't asking the right questions upfront, you're booking calls with people who can't buy.

Check: Does your funnel qualify before it books?
06

The Booking Friction

Every extra step between "interested" and "booked" costs you conversions.

Three clicks to book is three too many.

Check: How many clicks does it take a lead to book a call with you?
07

The No-Reactivation System

80% of leads that don't buy immediately are still in-market 3–6 months later.

Most businesses never follow up again.

Check: Do you have an automated reactivation sequence?
