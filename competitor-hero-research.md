## Pattern Analysis

### What every top competitor does in the hero:
1. **Specific ICP in the headline or sub** — not "businesses," but "B2B SaaS," "startups," "mid-sized businesses"
2. **Hard trust metrics** — not "great results" but "3.2x ROI," "+340% lead growth," "$1B+ revenue influenced"
3. **Two CTAs** — one action-ready ("Book a call") + one lower-commitment ("See results" / "Explore services")
4. **Social proof layer** — either client logos OR a Clutch/G2 badge OR both, directly in the hero
5. **Pain or fear hook** — several open with a problem statement, not a product statement

### What nobody is doing (Target Digital's opportunity):
- **AI agent stack transparency** — naming Claude Code, Railway, Graph MCP by name. No competitor names their actual toolchain in the hero. This is a differentiator.
- **"We are the agent jockeys"** — no competitor has claimed the operator identity. They all say "we use AI" not "we ARE the AI operators."
- **Cody Schneider framing** — replacing a team of dozens with 10 Claude instances is a concrete, viral-native story. No competitor has a story this specific.
- **Real deployment proof** — "pushed to Railway, running 24/7" is more concrete than any hero copy found in this research.

### Headline formulas in use:
| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Identity statement | "AI-Native Marketing Company" (Single Grain) |
| Pain opener | "SEO is hard to win" (Daydream) |
| Fear-of-missing-out | "Your competitors are already using AI. Are you?" (Power Reach AI) |
| Outcome-first | "Build a content engine... that drives pipeline, not just pageviews" (Growth Plays) |
| Double-benefit | "SEO that creates pipeline. GEO that positions your brand." (RevenueZen) |

## Recommendations for Target Digital Hero

**Current H1:** "YOUR FIRST AI MARKETING TEAM. HALF THE COST OF AN AGENCY."

**Strengths:** Price anchor is strong. "First AI marketing team" is differentiated.  
**Weakness:** "Half the cost" positions on price — cheapness framing. Competitors don't compete on price; they compete on outcome and identity.

**Alternative angles to test:**

1. *Identity angle:* "We Don't Run Campaigns. We Run Claude Code Agents."
2. *Outcome angle:* "10 AI Agents. Your Pipeline. Running 24/7."
3. *Fear angle:* "Your Competitors Just Replaced Their Marketing Team With AI."
4. *Story angle:* "Cody Schneider Did It. We Deploy the Same Stack For You."

**Trust badges to add to hero (currently missing):**
- "Deployed on Railway — 99% Uptime"
- "48hr average deployment"
- "Results in 7 days or less"

**CTA — add a secondary:**
- Primary: "Claim Your Free Growth Audit →"
- Secondary: "See How It Works" (scroll to process)
