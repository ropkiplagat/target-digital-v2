The 4-Step System

What we built for Joan — and every client after her.

01

Trigger-Based Review Requests

Every positive touchpoint — completed service, happy customer interaction, resolved ticket — triggers an automatic SMS or email review request within 24 hours. Strike while the experience is fresh.

02

Smart Review Routing

Happy customers go to Google or Facebook. Unhappy customers go to an internal feedback form first. You capture the problem before it becomes a public 1-star.

03

AI Response Management

Every review — positive or negative — gets a personalised AI response within 2 hours. Google rewards engagement. Customers see you care. Competitors don't respond at all.

04

Reputation Monitoring

Real-time alerts when new reviews land across Google, Facebook, and GMB. No surprises. No reviews slipping through unnoticed. Every platform, one dashboard.
